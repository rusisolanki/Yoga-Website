Our Story

We teach the art of mind body connection

Yogis go through rigorous certification in order to capably guide others towards enrichment of the physical & spiritual self. We take pride in building strong, knowledgable instructors, to spread the teachings of yoga.


Yoga Life Inspires, Educates, & Accelerates The Knowledge of Yoga Worldwide

Yoga Life is the international leader in online yoga teacher training, touching the lives of 75,000 yogis & providing the most resourceful training programs available today. As a pioneer & innovator in online training, our program format & mission of accessibility has completely changed the landscape of yoga teacher training & set the standard for online studios.

Our yoga teacher trainers are world class leaders in their respective fields, & our courses go deeper than simple practice to better enable our students as passionate business leaders.
